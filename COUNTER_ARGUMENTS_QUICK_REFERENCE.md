### **2. "Healthcare savings unproven (20-30% reduction)"**
**Rebuttal:** Twin Health achieved 40-60% reduction; we project half that (20-30%). Even at 10% reduction, ROI is still positive.

### **3. "QALY double-counting ($10B-$20B)"**
**Rebuttal:** QALY measures health outcomes (pain, mobility), not economic productivity. No overlap. Even eliminating all overlap, value is $13B.

---

### **4. "Can't attribute all improvements to Hayat AI"**
**Rebuttal:** We only claim incremental value above baseline. Baseline trend is worsening (healthspan gap increased 45%). Even at 50% attribution, value is $6.25B.

---

### **5. "No discount rate applied (NPV lower)"**
**Rebuttal:** Our $12.5B already uses 12% discount rate. At standard 10%, NPV is $14.5B (20% higher than our claim).
