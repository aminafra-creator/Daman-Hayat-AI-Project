# Hayat AI: Presentation Middle (5-Minute Script)

**Objective:** Transition from the visionary opening to the concrete solution and value calculation, building the case for the final "ask."

**Speaker:** Hayat AI Founder/CEO

**Associated Slides:** Slide 2 (Solution), Slide 3 (Value to Daman), Slide 4 (Value to Citizens), Slide 5 (Value to Abu Dhabi), Slide 6 (Total Worth)

---

## The Script

**(2:00 - 3:00 | The "How": Explaining the Digital Twin)**

*<Transition from opening line: "...Today, we will show you exactly how we achieve this future, together.">* 

"So, how do we make this future a reality? The answer lies in a proven, breakthrough technology called a Digital Twin.

Think of it as a virtual, living replica of your own body. It’s an AI-powered model that gets smarter every day, integrating data from six crucial sources: your genetics, your lab results, your wearable data, your medical imaging, your lifestyle, and even your family history. 

This isn't just data collection; it's deep, predictive intelligence. Our platform analyzes this information to see what the human eye can't—predicting the risk of chronic diseases like diabetes or heart disease 3 to 5 years *before* they happen. This gives us a critical window to intervene. To prevent, not just treat.

And this isn't experimental. The core technology is validated by over 20 years of research at institutions like Mount Sinai. And the business model is proven by companies like Twin Health, a metabolic digital twin platform recently valued at nearly a billion dollars. We are taking this proven technology and optimizing it for the unique genetics, culture, and healthcare system of the UAE."


**(3:00 - 4:15 | The Value to Daman: From Cost Center to Sustainable Partner)**

"For Daman, the impact is immediate and profound. Let’s start with the numbers.

By focusing on the highest-risk 27% of Thiqa members, we directly address the 60-70% of costs associated with preventable chronic disease. Our conservative projections show that for every dollar Daman invests in a member on our platform, it saves between two and three dollars in healthcare costs. 

At scale, this translates to over **one hundred million dollars in net savings annually**. Over ten years, the cumulative value to Daman is between **500 million and 1.5 billion dollars**.

This isn't just about cost savings. This is about transforming Daman’s business model for the future. It ensures long-term sustainability and solidifies your position as a global leader in proactive, AI-driven healthcare. It makes you a partner in your members' health, not just a payer for their sickness."


**(4:15 - 5:15 | The Value to Abu Dhabi's People: The Human Return)**

"But the most important return isn't measured in dollars. It's measured in years. Healthy years.

For the citizens of Abu Dhabi, this platform will add, conservatively, **150,000 healthy years of life** over the next decade. These are years a father gets to spend with his children. Years a grandmother remains active and independent. Years a skilled professional continues to contribute their expertise.

We will prevent thousands of cases of diabetes, heart attacks, and cancer. We will transform the lives of over 38,000 people in the first five years alone, and in doing so, protect the well-being of over 400,000 of their family members. This is the human return on investment—and it is the reason we are all in this room today."


**(5:15 - 6:30 | The Value to Abu Dhabi's Economy & The Big Reveal)**

"This human return fuels the economic engine of Abu Dhabi. A healthier population is a more productive workforce. The value of this increased productivity, combined with the direct healthcare savings, creates a **3 to 6 billion dollar positive impact** on Abu Dhabi's economy over the next decade. It directly supports the goals of Vision 2030 by creating a resilient, knowledge-based economy.

So, what is a solution like this actually worth?

When you combine the direct savings to Daman, the economic impact for Abu Dhabi, and the quality of life value for your citizens, the total value created by this partnership is between **12.5 and 26 billion dollars**.

Let that number sink in. This is the true scale of the opportunity before us."

## Speaker Notes & Delivery Cues

### **Part 1: The "How" (2:00-3:00)**
- **Tone:** Clear, confident, educational. Demystify the technology.
- **Pacing:** Methodical. Use the analogy of a "virtual, living replica."
- **Key phrases:** "Proven, breakthrough technology," "predicting... 3 to 5 years *before* they happen," "proven by companies like Twin Health."
- **Body Language:** Use hands to show the six data sources coming together. Confident, stable posture.
- **AV Cue:** Slide 2 is up. Animate the flow diagram: 1. Data Collection → 2. AI Analysis → 3. Personalized Plan. Show logos of Mount Sinai and Twin Health as trust signals.

### **Part 2: Value to Daman (3:00-4:15)**
- **Tone:** Business-focused, impactful. You are speaking directly to their bottom line.
- **Pacing:** Start slow with "Let's start with the numbers," then build intensity.
- **Key phrases:** "Immediate and profound," "save between two and three dollars," "over **one hundred million dollars in net savings annually**," "**1.5 billion dollars**."
- **Body Language:** Direct eye contact with the key financial decision-makers. Use a hand gesture to show a rising graph of savings.
- **AV Cue:** Transition to Slide 3. Animate the key numbers: "$100M+ Annually," "$1.5B Total Value." Show the ROI bar chart clearly illustrating cost vs. savings.

### **Part 3: The Human Return (4:15-5:15)**
- **Tone:** Empathetic, visionary, heartfelt. This is the emotional anchor.
- **Pacing:** Slow down significantly. Let the weight of the numbers sink in.
- **Key phrases:** "The most important return isn't measured in dollars," "**150,000 healthy years of life**," "protect the well-being of over 400,000 of their family members."
- **Body Language:** More open, hands to the heart or in a gesture of encompassing the community. Look around the room.
- **AV Cue:** Transition to Slide 4. Show the "150,000 Healthspan Years Gained" number prominently. Use inspiring, relatable images of Emirati families.

### **Part 4: The Big Reveal (5:15-6:30)**
- **Tone:** Authoritative, conclusive, building to a powerful climax.
- **Pacing:** Start with economic facts, then pause dramatically before the final valuation.
- **Key phrases:** "**3 to 6 billion dollar positive impact**," "So, what is a solution like this actually worth?" (PAUSE), "**12.5 to 26 billion dollars**."
- **Body Language:** Stand tall. Use a sweeping gesture for the economic impact. Then, pause, bring your hands together, and deliver the final number with immense gravity and confidence.
- **AV Cue:** Transition to Slide 5, showing the economic impact chart. Then, transition to Slide 6 for the final reveal. Animate the numbers for Daman, Abu Dhabi, and Citizens, then have them combine into the massive "$26B" total.

### **The Transition to the "Ask":**
- After the line "...the true scale of the opportunity before us," you will pause for 3-5 seconds. Let the number hang in the air. The audience will be thinking, "This must cost a fortune."
- This is the perfect setup to transition to Slide 7, where you reveal the tiny ask, creating maximum psychological impact. You've anchored them to $26 billion, so the $15-20 million ask will feel incredibly small and reasonable.
