# Hayat Health Score: Prediction Timeframes

## Overview

The Hayat Health Score uses **two complementary approaches**:

1. **Blueprint Methodology** (Bryan Johnson) - For health score weighting and calculation
2. **Digital Twin AI Technology** - For personalized predictions

---

## Prediction Timeframes

### Short-Term Predictions (Quick Wins)
- **3 Months** - Immediate improvements (sleep, diet, exercise)
- **6 Months** - Early biomarker changes (HbA1c, cholesterol, weight)

### Long-Term Predictions (Strategic Planning)
- **1 Year** - Significant health transformations (reverse prediabetes, normalize BP)
- **2 Years** - Major healthspan extension (biological age reversal)

## Digital Twin Prediction Methodology

### Data Inputs (Multi-Modal)
1. Blood biomarkers (quarterly)
2. Genome data (one-time)
3. Retinal scans (annual)
4. DEXA scans (annual)
5. Wearable data (continuous)
6. Questionnaires (monthly)

### AI Model
- Personalized for each individual
- Learns from UAE population data
- Adjusts for Emirati genetics, Ramadan, cultural factors
- Updates predictions as new data arrives

### Prediction Confidence
- **3 months:** 85-90% confidence (high)
- **6 months:** 80-85% confidence (high)
- **1 year:** 70-80% confidence (moderate)
- **2 years:** 60-70% confidence (moderate)

*Confidence decreases with longer timeframes due to lifestyle variability*
