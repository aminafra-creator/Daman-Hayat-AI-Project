### **What We Know About Thiqa:**

**Source:** Multiple insurance industry reports and government websites

1. **Thiqa is managed by Daman Insurance**
   - Exclusively for UAE nationals in Abu Dhabi
   - Funded by Abu Dhabi government
   - No annual benefit limit per member
   - Comprehensive coverage (inpatient + outpatient)

2. **Coverage Statistics:**
   - **98% of UAE nationals** in Abu Dhabi have medical insurance
   - Thiqa covers approximately **27% of all government insurance contracts** (as of end 2022)
   - Source: PMC study (2024)

3. **No Public Budget Data Found:**
   - ‚ùå Thiqa-specific budget NOT disclosed in public documents
   - ‚ùå Abu Dhabi emirate-level healthcare budget NOT found in federal sources
   - ‚ùå Number of Thiqa members NOT officially published

## üìä UAE HEALTHCARE MARKET DATA (VERIFIED)

**Source:** Mordor Intelligence, Business Wire, Yahoo Finance (2024-2025)

### **Total UAE Health Insurance Market:**
- **2024 Market Size:** USD 10.51 billion
- **2025 Market Size:** USD 9.34-10.51 billion (different sources)
- **2030 Projected:** USD 13.6-18.83 billion
- **CAGR:** 7.8-9.5%

### **Key Players:**
- **Daman:** Largest insurance payer in Abu Dhabi, 3rd largest in Dubai
- Manages Thiqa program on behalf of Abu Dhabi government
- Also manages "Enaya" (Dubai government employee scheme)
